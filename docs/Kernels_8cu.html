Main simulation kernel. This is where the magic happens.

Parameters
I_atmArray containing blackbody intensity of atmosphere, in SI units.
I_gndArray containing blackbody intensity of ground, in SI units.
I_telArray containing blackbody intensity of telescope, in SI units.
sigoutArray for storing output power, for each channel, for each time, in SI units.
flagoutArray for storing wether beam is in chop A or B, in nod AB or BA.
freqs_srcArray containing bin frequencies, in Hertz.
azsrcArray containing Azimuth co-ordinates of source, in degrees.
elsrcArray containing Elevation co-ordinates of source, in degrees.
eta_apArray containing aperture efficiencies, for each bin frequency.
x_atmArray containing x co-ordinates of the atmosphere screen, in meters.
y_atmArray containing y co-ordinates of the atmosphere screen, in meters.
PWV_screenArray containing PWV value of atmosphere, over the range described by x_atm and y_atm, in millimeters.
freqs_atmArray containing frequencies over which to interpolate atmospheric transmission, in Hertz.
PWV_atmArray containing PWV values over which to interpolate atmospheric transmission, in millimeters.
eta_atmArray with transmission parameters as fuiunction of freqs_atm and PWV_atm.
filterbankArray containing filterbank of instrument.
sourceArray containing source intensity, as function of azsrc, elsrc and freqs_src, in SI units.
stateArray with states for drawing random Gaussian values for noise calculations.
