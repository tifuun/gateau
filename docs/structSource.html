double* Source::I_nu

Flat array of specific intensities, indexed as [i * ni + k * ni * nj + j]. Here, i is axis 0 (Az), j axis 1 (El) and k axis 2 (frequency). Note that size of I_nu = nAz * nEl * nfreqs.
